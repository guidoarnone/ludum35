Wlobbie





Description

This is a small game for the Ludum Dare Jam 35. This year's theme was "Shapeshift".

In a house that no one dares to enter, Wlobbie is following the legend of a treasure. The problem is that the house has its secrets. It changes.

If you're on Chrome, UnityWebPlayer won't work unless you do "chrome://flags/#enable-npapi" first. You can disable this after playing.

Controls

  • Move with WASD
  • Right click or press E to interact with the enviroment.
